Council-Based Evaluation Architecture

Three expert modules support a final council synthesis.

The evaluation system is organized around independent expert review for safety and adversarial robustness, governance and compliance reasoning, and humanitarian and operational context. Each module produces an independent score on a 0.0–5.0 scale, and the council orchestrator synthesizes the final composite result.
