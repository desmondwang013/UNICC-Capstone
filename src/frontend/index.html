Council-Based Evaluation Architecture

Three expert modules support a final council synthesis.

Content submitted to the Safety Lab is routed through three independent expert judges — adversarial red-teaming, governance & risk classification, and regulatory compliance. Each produces an independent score, then the council orchestrator compares, critiques, arbitrates, and synthesizes a final composite verdict before any AI agent is cleared for UNICC deployment.

Council Orchestrator

Final composite verdict

The council orchestrator serves as the final synthesis layer. It compares outputs from the three expert modules, critiques differences in reasoning, arbitrates conflicting findings, and applies a conservative tier override — the final risk tier is never lower than the highest individual judge verdict. Disagreements are surfaced explicitly as governance signals, not suppressed.
